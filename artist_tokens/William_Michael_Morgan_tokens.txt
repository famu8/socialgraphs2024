william michael morgan born may 13 1993 is an american country music singer from vicksburg mississippi he was signed to warner bros nashvillehis debut single i met a girl reached number two on the country airplay chart and number eight on the hot country songs chart in october 2016 the song was cowritten with sam hunt the albums second single missing was released to country radio on november 7 2016 his debut studio album vinyl was released on september 30 2016 morgan parted ways with warner bros nashville in january 2019
