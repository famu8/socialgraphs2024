chalee tennison born april 11 1969 is an american country music artist from the state of texas she has released three majorlabel studio albums two on asylum records and one on dreamworks records in addition to charting six singles on the billboard hot country singles tracks now hot country songs charts her highest charting singles just because she lives there and go back both reached no 36 on that chartchalee tennison was born in texas and was influenced musically by her brothers collection of 8track tapes which included artists such as tammy wynette and tanya tucker she sang in church time she also worked a variety of jobs including waitress substitute teacher and prison security guardshe moved to nashville tennessee and worked as a demo singer after record producer jerry taylor discovered her demos he helped her sign to asylum records in 1999 her first selftitled album was issued that year with someone elses turn to cry as the lead single following it were handful of water
