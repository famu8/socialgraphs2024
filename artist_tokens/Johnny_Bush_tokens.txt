john bush shinn iii february 17 1935 – october 16 2020 was an american country music singer songwriter and musician nicknamed the country caruso bush was best known for his distinctive voice and for writing the song whiskey river a top 10 hit for himself which also became the signature song of fellow country artist willie nelson he was especially popular in his native texasbush was born john bush shinn iii in the kashmere gardens neighborhood of houston he listened to the western swing music of bob wills and his texas playboys and the honkytonk sounds of artists such as ernest tubb lefty frizzell and hank thompson his uncle the host of a local radio program on ktht urged bush and his brother to play on air giving bush his first experience of performing in public bush subsequently moved to san antonio in 1952 beginning a solo career in area honkytonks such as the texas star inn before switching to drums during this period he earned his stage name when an announcer mistakenly
