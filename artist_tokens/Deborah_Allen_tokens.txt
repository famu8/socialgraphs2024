deborah allen born deborah lynn thurmond on september 30 1953 is an american country music singer and songwriter since 1976 allen has issued 12 albums and charted 14 singles on the billboard hot country songs chart she recorded the 1983 crossover hit baby i lied which reached no 4 on the country chart and no 26 on the billboard hot 100 allen has also written no 1 singles for herself janie
