country musics girls night out superstar women of country the trio have released four studio albums for sony music nashvillepistol annies made their debut on april 22 2011 on the academy of country musics girls night out superstar women of country on cbs performing hell on heels the women gave themselves the nicknames lonestar annie lambert hippie annie monroe and holler annie presley they
