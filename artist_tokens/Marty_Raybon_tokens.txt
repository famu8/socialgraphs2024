marlon marty raybon born december 8 1959 is an american country music artist he is known primarily for his role as the lead singer of the country band shenandoah a role which he held from 1985 to 1997 until he rejoined the band in 2014 he recorded his first solo album marty raybon in 1995 on sparrow records before leaving shenandoah in 1997 he and his brother tim formed a duo known as the raybon brothers which had crossover success that year with the hit single butterfly kissesthe raybon brothers split up in 1997 and marty raybon resumed his career as a solo artist a second selftitled album was released in 2000 followed by 2003s full circle 2006 saw the release of when the sand runs out which included the single shenandoah saturday night
