raybon brothers were an american country music duo from sanford florida consisting of brothers marty raybon and tim raybon prior to the duos inception in 1997 marty raybon was the lead singer of the country music band shenandoah having left in 1997 before rejoining in 2014the raybon brothers charted in 1997 with a cover of bob carlisles pop hit butterfly kisses their version was a top 40 hit on both the billboard country music charts and the billboard hot 100 a second single the way shes lookin reached the lower regions of the country charts marty recorded a number of solo projects before rejoining shenandoah
