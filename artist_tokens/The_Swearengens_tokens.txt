the swearengens are an american alternative country band formed in 2010 in seattlethe band consists of fredd luongo on vocals and guitar soren godbersen on guitars and cadillacs etc forrest marowitz on bass and backing vocals and jesse harmonson on drums all of their songs are written by frontman fredd they can often be found playing and sipping a beverage at places in seattle like the tractor tavern the conor byrne pub and the little red henthe band has been described as from epic road songs to tearstained tales of heartache from texasstyle rockers to darkhumored murder ballads the swearengens cover a lot of musical and emotional territory in their twisted attempt at reviving the glory days altcountry of whiskeytown the jayhawks dwight yoakam uncle tupelo and steve earlethe band released their first album devil gets her way on june 30 2012 it was named one of seattles best country albums of 2012 by seattle weekly their second album waiting on the sunrise was released on june 8 2013 it was named one of the top ten albums of 2013 by leigh bezezekoff at kexpfm the bands most recent album merican woman other tales was released on june 26 2015
