edens edge was an american country music band founded by hannah blaylock lead vocals dean berner vocals guitar dobro and cherrill green vocals mandolin banjo guitar the band was signed to big machine records which released their selftitled debut album it included the singles amen and too good to be true the group disbanded in 2013 when blaylock departedall three members of edens edge are natives of arkansas they won a contest sponsored by cmt in 2006 where they met nashville songwriter kye fleming they moved to nashville in 2007 the group eventually signed a record deal with big machine records in april 2010 and the trios debut single amen was released in april 2011 it has become the groups first top 20 hit their selftitled debut album was released on june 12 2012 too good to be true was released as the albums second and final single on april 9 2012in march 2013 it was announced that edens edge had parted ways with big machine records and that member hannah blaylock had left the band blaylock auditioned for the voice in 2018 but failed to make a team
