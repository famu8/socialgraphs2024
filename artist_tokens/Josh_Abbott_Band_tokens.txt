josh abbott band is a texas country band originally from lubbock composed of josh abbott austin david banjo edward villanueva percussion david fralin keysmandolin jimmy hartman bass guitar harmony vocals kris farrow electric guitar harmony vocals adam hill fiddle and cale richardson guitar former recent members include preston wait fiddle guitar steel guitar and caleb keeter guitar josh abbott founded the band in 2006 in lubbock texas while attending texas tech university where he was a member of the texas epsilon chapter of phi delta theta he founded the band while in grad school with fraternity brothers austin davis neel huey and andrew hurt the band recorded a 4song demo including taste in 2007 along with fiddler preston wait and began touring in 2008 huey and hurt dropped out to pursue careers while preston wait and eddie villanueva officially joined the band then recorded their debut album “ scapegoat ” in 2008 as well caleb keeter and james hertless joined in 2010 with
