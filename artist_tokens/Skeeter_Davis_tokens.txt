skeeter davis born mary frances penick december 30 1931 – september 19 2004 was an american country music singer and songwriter who sang crossover pop music songs including 1962s the end of the world she started out as part of the davis sisters as a teenager in the late 1940s eventually landing on rca victor in the late 1950s she became a solo starone of the first women to achieve major stardom in the country music field as a solo vocalist she was an acknowledged influence on tammy wynette and dolly parton and was hailed as an extraordinary countrypop singer by the new york times music critic
